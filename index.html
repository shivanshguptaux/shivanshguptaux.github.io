About Me

My name is Shivansh Gupta and I'm a designer with a knack for creating out-of-the-box and innovative ideas. Throughout my academic career at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad, I pursued my interests in film making, photography, and graphic design, which I continued to explore as a freelance graphic designer and filmmaker for a few years. In addition, I have also had the opportunity to work as a teacher of physics at a leading educational institution, where I developed my skills in communication and problem-solving.

Recently, I discovered the field of UX design and have been thoroughly enjoying the process of creating user-centered designs that are not just visually appealing, but also solve real problems. I am excited to continue growing in this field and am eager to transition into a full-time UX/UI designer.

When I’m not designing, you can find me indulging in intense chess matches, exploring the creative endless possibilities of street photography and listening to songs. :)

If you're looking for someone who's equal parts creative and professional, give me a shout!
